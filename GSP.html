Describe the transformation

The Green Square Project is an $8 billion investment on a 14 hectare block of land. The space will hold a public library and plaza, aquatic and recreation centre, community cultural precinct, parks and public art. There are also various private developments including retail and hotel infrastructure that will generate revenue for the area. These public centres feature two architecturally designed spaces, the public library and aquatic center, in order to generate more functional community spaces and also increase human activity within the area.

Above and Underground Design: Most of the entire library space was moved underground completely and within the small rectangular building, and this allows a lot more open space above ground for the public to congregate on.
