George Street Light Rail


The NSW Government has begun work on a $2.1 billion light rail line, connecting Randwick with Circular Quay.
This is to provide the community with easy transport options to key areas such as The Rocks and Circular Quay to the city centre's retail heart and down to Chinatown.
