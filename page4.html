What is Data Journalism?

Data journalism is the use of data, specifically numerical data in journalism to reveal, better explain and/or provide context to a news story. It often relies on the use of charts, graphs and infographics while interacting with several other fields such as statistics and computer science.

Is CityRail providing Quality Service to their Customers?

In many cities, transportation is the key to keeping a city moving. It is what allows individuals to get from A to B within a short timeframe and grants individuals the ability to work in a place far from home. The Sydney railway network is currently at capacity as of 2018 due to the fact that their railway system and infrastructure has not been expanded enough over the last 50 years. As a result, customers are experiencing indefinite delays on multiple lines.
