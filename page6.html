Data Driven Design



The Problem

Delays are a major problem identified in the data visualisation whereby hotspot stations experience massive delays exceeding 100 minutes of waiting time. These delays can result in frustration, congestion and unemployment which will damage the economy of NSW in the long run. As a result, action must be taken to improve these train stations to decrease delays and improve overall customer service. The main reasons why delays occur are due to trespassing, vandalism, poor weather conditions, trackwork and cable theft.
