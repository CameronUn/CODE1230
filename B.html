Describe the transformation

The Barangaroo project is an incredibly large urban transformation that went from a once busy shipyard to an urban sprawl of public facilities and private enterprise. The space is divided into 3 precincts, all containing these main components of an urban transformation. Barangaroo South will house the business side or
financial services sector, which includes office buildings, shops, apartments, public venues, a hotel and a Wynyard transport link. Barangaroo Central houses a range of commercial spaces such as a new casino, with various new public facilities, mainly parks and public spaces where community venues can be held. Finally Barangaroo reserve is a 6 hectare open parkland available to the public, this spot features a pedestrian track that loops all the way to the rocks, and presents various lookout spots and places to hold public events. Barangaroo is an incredibly large complex, that is still in development, mainly the central location, which will generate large amounts of activity and profit, but the south and reserve are complete and have been very successful.
